Our Curriculum

We offer a diverse and well-structured curriculum that aims to provide students with both academic excellence and holistic development.

  • Ideal Pop-up Kits designed for foundational learning
  • Fun activities including rhymes, storytelling, art
  • Focus on motor skills and emotional growth
  • Books: Learnwell Smart Term Books (CBSE-aligned)
  • Subjects: English, Maths, EVS, GK, Hindi, Gujarati
  • Value Education & foundational computer literacy

  • Subjects: English, Maths, Science, SST, Hindi, Gujarati, Sanskrit, GK

  • Subjects: English, Hindi, Gujarati, Sanskrit, Maths, Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), SST

  • Annual Day, Sports Meet, Talent Shows
  • Environmental and Social Awareness Drives
  • Unit Tests, Term Exams, Oral Exams
  • Formative & Summative Assessments per semester
  • Competitions & Academic Refresher Programs
