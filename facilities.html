Our Facilities

Bus Facility

Our school operates five modern buses with a capacity of 80 students each, covering all major routes in Bhachau and surrounding areas. All buses are equipped with GPS tracking, first aid kits, and trained attendants for student safety.

Computer Lab

Our state-of-the-art computer lab features 40 high-speed computers with the latest software, high-speed internet, and interactive learning tools. Students receive hands-on training in programming, digital literacy, and computer applications.

Chemistry Lab

The well-equipped chemistry lab allows students to perform experiments safely under expert supervision. It contains all necessary chemicals, apparatus, and safety equipment to facilitate practical learning as per CBSE guidelines.

Security Measures

Our school prioritizes student safety with a secure campus featuring CCTV cameras and controlled entry and exit points, ensuring a protected environment for learning and growth.

Playground

Our school boasts expansive playgrounds equipped with top-notch facilities for a range of sports and activities, fostering physical fitness, teamwork, and holistic development in our students.

Medical Room

Basic medical care and first aid available for students.

Sports

We have excellent sports facilities including a playground for cricket, kabadi, volleyball, and athletics; Regular inter-school competitions are organized.

Open Stage & Space

Our school features a dynamic open stage and event space, providing students with a platform to showcase their talents, creativity, and confidence through performances, presentations, and various events.
